Projects Showcase

BookMe was our Final Project, developed collaboratively by a team of four. This platform consists of general users seeking a service, and providers, offering a service (time-based). Providers sign up, select their monthly subscription plan, connect their google/microsoft calendar (2 way sync) to the platform, set their availability, their fee, and add tags and labels to their profile. This is based on which they can be searched/filtered and found by location, city, country, language, expertise, experience and so on. Impact Statements: Ability to authenticate via Google. Sign Up Users to become a Provider. Ability to delete Profile to comply with GDPR. Google Calendar Integration. Ability to sign up to a Subscription Plan. Technical Specifications: React Node Express Postgres Sequelize Google Authentication Google Calendar Appointment Schedule Material UI
